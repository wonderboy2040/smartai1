============================================================
PORT FORWARDING SETUP (REQUIRED FOR EXTERNAL ACCESS)
============================================================
Step 1: Open browser → go to http://192.168.1.1 (router login)
Step 2: Login (admin/admin or as set on router sticker)
Step 3: Find "Port Forwarding" or "Virtual Server"
Step 4: Add rule:
  - External Port: 8080
  - Internal IP:   192.168.0.104
  - Internal Port: 8080
  - Protocol:      TCP
Step 5: Save & Apply

After this, ANY device anywhere can open:
  http://103.2.234.170:8080

============================================================
ALTERNATIVE — CLOUDFLARE TUNNEL (NO PORT FORWARDING)
============================================================
If router setup is not possible:
  1. Download cloudflared from cloudflare.com
  2. Run: cloudflared tunnel --url http://localhost:8080
  3. You get a https://xxxx.trycloudflare.com URL
  4. That URL works anywhere, no port forwarding needed
